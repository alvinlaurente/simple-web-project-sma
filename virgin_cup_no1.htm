Editor's Choice
Shirley Temple



Virgin Cup No.1

    Ingredients
  • 6 Parts Ginger Ale
  • 1 Part Lemon Juice
  • 1 Part Lime Juice
  • 3 Leaves Basil
  • 4 Slices Cucumber
  • 1 Sprig Mint Leaf
    How to mix
  • Fill a highball glass with crushed ice.
  • Add all ingredients and stir it.
  • Garnish with cucumber and mint leaf.
